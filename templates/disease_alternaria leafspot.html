Alternaria Leafspot

Symptoms

Symptoms caused by A. arachidis:

Lesions produced by A. arachidis are brown in color and irregular in shape surrounded by yellowish halos.

Symptoms caused by A. tenuissima:

Symptoms produced by A. tenuissima are characterized by blighting of apical portions of leaflets which turn light to dark brown color. In the later stages of infection, blighted leaves curl inward and become brittle.

Management

Foliar application of:

Mancozeb (0.3%) or Copper oxychloride (0.3%) or Carbendazim(0.1%)
